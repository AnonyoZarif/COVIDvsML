Defeat COVID-19 using ML!

COVID vs. ML
With the rising infections of COVID-19 coronavirus in Bangladesh, people are rushing to their nearest hospitals to test for the disease. But due to the surge of both infected and non-infected people in the testing centers, a lot of new exposure to the virus is occurring. This machine learning/AI-based tool prevents that by testing for COVID with quite reliable accuracy only from an x-ray or the audio recording of the patient's cough. This, paired with the self evaluation of symptoms and self-quarantining of suspected patients, we can defeat COVID much easier.
